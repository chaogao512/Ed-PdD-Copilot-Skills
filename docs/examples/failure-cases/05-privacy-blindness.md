# Failure Case 05: Privacy Blindness

## Weak input

“我们收集学生课堂视频、发言、表情和作业数据，训练模型自动判断学生学习状态，并把结果推送给班主任和学院领导。”

## Expected skill response

Use `governance-idea-evaluator`, `mixed-methods-evidence-template` or `edtech-pre-submission-reviewer`.

## Expected diagnosis

- **CRITICAL: Ethics impossible unless redesigned**. The input involves high-risk multimodal student data and possible profiling without consent, minimization, access control or appeal.
- **CRITICAL: Human role erased**. Automated judgment is pushed to managers without interpretation, review or correction.
- **MAJOR: Evidence mismatch**. Behavioral signals cannot directly prove learning state without validation and context.

## Required repair

Narrow the scenario, minimize data, obtain authorization and consent, anonymize or desensitize records, add teacher interpretation, provide student explanation and appeal routes, and downgrade the claim to “risk signal requiring human review”.
