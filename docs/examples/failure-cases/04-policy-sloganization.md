# Failure Case 04: Policy Sloganization

## Weak input

“国家高度重视教育数字化，因此本文构建一个 AI 教育治理模型，推动教育高质量发展。”

## Expected skill response

Use `edtech-intro-drafter` or `edtech-pre-submission-reviewer`.

## Expected diagnosis

- **MAJOR: Policy sloganization**. Policy is used as a motivational slogan rather than an analytical requirement.
- **MAJOR: Missing contradiction**. The text does not name the governance bottleneck or stakeholder pain.

## Required repair

Translate policy into a concrete demand: data interoperability, process transparency, inclusive support, teacher capacity or AI safety. Then connect it to a specific contradiction such as data islands or digital formalism.
